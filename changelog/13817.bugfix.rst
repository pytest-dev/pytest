:func:`~pytest.Parser.addoption` now raises a clear ``ValueError`` when called with an option name that does not start with ``-`` or ``--``, instead of crashing later with ``AttributeError`` or silently registering the name as a positional argument. Common typos like ``parser.addoption("shuffle")`` now fail with a traceback pointing at the offending ``conftest.py`` and a "did you mean ``--shuffle``?" suggestion.
